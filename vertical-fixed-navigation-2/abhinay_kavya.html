Kavya

A poet is a person who is passionately in love with the language. So, if you are brimming with buried feelings or thoughts that are needed to be heard, weave them into beautiful words and read them aloud.

Rules

1. This module consists of one round.
2. Participants will be given a poem and also one poem has to be
self-prepared.
3. Language used can be Hindi or Marathi only.
4. Half hour preparation time will be given. Participants can
carry the poem while performing.
5. Participants have to report 1 hour before the competition.
6. Registration fees: Rs 100/person
7. Team size : Solo event
8. Time duration : 4-5min
