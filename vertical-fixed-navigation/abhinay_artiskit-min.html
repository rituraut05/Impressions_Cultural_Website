Rules

- 1. This Module consists of single round.
2. Teams are required to submit a short synopsis (< 150 words) of the
script mentioning the script writer, director and the cast of their play 5
days prior to event for censoring.
3. Language of the play should be Hindi, Marathi or English (short sub
passages of other languages are allowed).
4. Adapted script should be mentioned beforehand else the team will
be debarred from the competition.
5. Participants would be provided with 2 tables, 4 chairs and a level.
6. Individual properties are allowed based on the discretion of
organizers.
7. Participants would be provided with a laptop for music insertions.
The sound tracks should be brought in a PenDrive or CD in basic
formats (participants should pre-check their tracks).
8. Basic light system (general light, left and right spots) and basic
music system will be provided.
9. Registration fees: Rs 300/Team
10. No. of participants per team: 2-8 (+ 1 lights and 1 sound).
11. Time duration : 15min ( +3 min stage setup time)
